# Moonshot
A game about golfing on the moon

# Core Idea

## Theme
MOON

## Setting 
The driving range at the moon's most popular (public) golf course.

## Genre
An idler game where the player is slowly working toward awakening a big moon monster who is going to ruin everyone's day.


# Core Mechanics
1. Score - Score is earned passively. The player avatar is consistently shooting balls at a fixed rate.
2. Player upgrades - The player can buy upgrades with accumulated score ultimately increase the rate of score earned per second. There are various upgrade types that affect the balls being shot by the player avatar. Examples include increased speed/distance, balls the split up, balls that are larger/faster.
3. Enemies - Enemies will not be present at the onset of the game but will be a purchasable feature in the beginning tier of progression. Enemies hit with balls will net more score. Enemy spawn rate is a purchasable upgrade 
4. Win condition - The game ends when the player reaches a certain score and the cosmic horror eats the moon.
